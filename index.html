How to use this Visualisation

This presentation is composed of 3 different parts: - Map of the AOI with some land use data. - Data insight where you will be able to see the different ratio of forest destroy for each time reference. - A range slider that you can manipulate in order to change the time reference and see the evolution of the land use and forest detruction years after years
