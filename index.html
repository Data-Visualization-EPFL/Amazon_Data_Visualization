Context

The Amazonie is the largest and most biodiverse tract of tropical rainforest in the world. It has a primary role in world ecosystem by participating in the regulation of CO2 concentration in the atmosphere and by being home for a very large number of vegetal and animal species. But as you will see, the fragil natural environment is subjec to destruction, a major part of that is due to the land use strategy of the three country sharing this area: Bolivia, Peru, Brazil.

How to use this Visualisation

This presentation is composed of 3 different parts:
- A Map of the area of interest with some land use data such as the agriculture, the water present...
- Data insight where you will be able to see the different ratio of forest destroy for each time reference.
